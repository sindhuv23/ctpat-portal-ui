SVI Agreement


We, the undersigned, consent to the addition of our company name to the CTPAT application.
The CTPAT application allows consenting certified CTPAT partners to verify the participation status of other consenting certified CTPAT partners.
To gain access, each partner must have consented to the release of their company name among the CTPAT membership.The CTPAT application is an Internet, web-based point of electronic access limited to assenting certified CTPAT Partners. The CTPAT application is found on the http://www.cbp.gov web site and controlled and maintained by CBP.
In order to participate, a company officer must electronically sign the SVI agreement.
